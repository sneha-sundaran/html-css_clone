We are

ROOTS

The Roots Group was founded by Mr. K. Ramasamy, a socially conscious visionary with a mission to deliver world-class products and services. A firm believer in the adage that "Simplicity is the key to efficiency", he remains the harbinger of change within the organization.

The foundation of the Roots Group is steeped in the legacy of sound. The journey began with a mission to create a resilient, powerful, and world-class automotive horn. What has followed is history. The Roots Group consists of diversified entities and has a leadership presence across industrial cleaning, automotive components, precision equipment & cast components.

Education, Healthcare, and Spiritual harmony form part of the group’s beyond-commerce initiatives.
